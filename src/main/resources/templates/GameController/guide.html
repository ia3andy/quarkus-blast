Guide

Quarkus Blast is made with Quarkus server-side rendering and htmx.

  • 6 Quark types (up, down, charm, strange, top, bottom) with different charges.
  • Tap a Quark to select it, then tap another Quark to swap their positions.
  • If there are adjacent Quarks of the same type, a blast reaction is triggered!
  • Make big reactions for maximum points!
  • Watch out for combos! If multiple blast reactions occur simultaneously, it's a combo, yielding even more points.
  • No possible reaction, tap the same Quark twice to make it blast.

Aim to blast it all and conquer the leaderboard!
