Guide

Quarkus Blast is an exciting game that pushes the limits of Quarkus server-side rendering (Renarde, Qute and Web Bundler) with htmx.

  • You'll encounter 6 Quark types (up, down, charm, strange, top, bottom) with different charges on a grid waiting for you to tap into their power..
  • Tap a Quark to select it, then tap another Quark to swap their positions.
  • Keep your eyes peeled for clusters of the same Quark type adjacent to each other because when they align, a blast reaction is triggered! Each blast reaction generates points based on the total charge and the number of Quarks involved.
  • Watch out for combos! If multiple blast reactions occur simultaneously, it's a combo, yielding even more points.
  • No possible reaction, tap the same Quark twice to make it blast.

Your goal is to maximize your score by strategically swapping Quarks, triggering blast reactions, and unleashing powerful combos. Aim to blast it all and conquer the leaderboard!
